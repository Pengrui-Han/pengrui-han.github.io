AI for Mind and Brain

Using AI as a scientific instrument for cognition and the brain

AI has become a powerful scientific instrument, increasingly used to analyze data and accelerate discovery across the sciences. I am drawn to questions that span basic science (how does the brain support language? how do reasoning and decision-making break down?) and applied science (how can computational tools help us understand and eventually support conditions like aphasia, Alzheimer’s disease, and other disorders of language and memory?). I aim to use AI models and algorithms to make progress on both.
